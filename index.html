DON'T PLAY WITH THE FIRE ...


Fire is the production of a flame and the visible degradation of a body by an oxidation chemical reaction called combustion.

For several years, Lydia has been playing with fire. She tames the flames and controls them to a completely crazy music. Sometimes Lydia loses control and becomes mindless, irrational ... Smell at your hair, it could be that it smells like a barbecue!

Are you burning with impatience?

KNIFE THROWING


Chris is big and strong, his precision will (or won't) save your life. Since the youngest age he hunts with blades.

His assistant Lyn has already paid the price, will she end up alive at the end of this issue? Or completely pierced with knifes? It would be a pity to destroy this body of a princess.

Come and discover this show and open your eyes.

BROTHERS LAKAN


They will never speak and yet you will be frightened!

With a father and mother completely crazy, how not to become one? After having assassinated their parents, the brothers decided to leave the family home and travel the world.
When the Lakan brothers arrive in the city, suspicious disappearances go on TV...

Are they only three? Be careful, you may be the next!
